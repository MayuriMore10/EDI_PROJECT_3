What is EDI 810?

The X12 Invoice message standard for electronic billing. Communicates seller/buyer information, invoice details, line items, pricing, charges, and totals between trading partners.

Purpose: Electronic invoice transmission between trading partners
Standard: X12 EDI Transaction Set 810
Key Data: Invoice numbers, dates, line items, pricing, totals
